Potato Wedges

Description

Delicious oven-baked seasoned potato wedges. Pairs well with burgers. Yields 4 servings and takes about 35 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 russet potato
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef(80% lean)
      • Misc. seasonings
    • Onion powder
    • Garlic powder
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • Optional parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F while cutting potatos.
  2. Cut potato in half lengthwise.
  3. Cut potato halves lengthwise.
  4. Cut potato quarters lengthwise
  5. Season cut potatos and bake in oven for about 25 minutes or until they are easily pierced with a fork.
  6. Pair with delicious burgers.
