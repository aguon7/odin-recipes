Sweet Tea

Description

A perfect southern classic for a hot summer day. Yields 7 servings and takes about 3 hours to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of boiling water
  • 6 cups of cool water
  • 6 tea bags
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 pinch of baking soda

Directions

  1. Sprinkle pinch of baking soda into a 64-ounch heat-proof glass pitcher.
  2. Pour boiling water into pitcher and then add the tea bags.
  3. After 15 minutes of steeping, remove bags and stir in sugar until dissolved.
  4. Pour in cool water and refrigerate for about 3 hours or until cold.
