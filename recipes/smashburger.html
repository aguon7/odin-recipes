Smashburgers

Description

Delicious, crispy, and juicy smashburger recipe. Yields 4 burgers and takes about 20 minutes to prepare.

Recommended minimum ground beef fat percentage is 80/20.

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef(80% lean)
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened, or as needed
  • 4 6-inch squares parchment paper
  • salt for taste
  • burger toppings of choice

Directions

  1. Preheat grill on high heat with a lightly oiled grate.
  2. Place a cast iron flat-top griddle or large cast iron skillet onto the grill.
  3. Let griddle or skillet preheat until smoking.
  4. Spread butter on inside of buns and toast them until lightly browned.
  5. Shape ground beef into 8 2-ounce balls. Pack the meat lightly.
  6. Place a ball on the flat top and cover with parchment. (Reuse the parchment for later balls.)
  7. Smash ball down to 1/4 inch thickness with 2 study spatulas in a criss-crossed fashion.
  8. Grill for approximately 45 seconds or until edges are dark brown and centers are light pink.
  9. Use a spatula or bench scraper to gently remove the patties and flip a patty over.
  10. Place cheese on the patty after flipping and grill for an additional 15 to 20 seconds.
  11. Remove finished patties and assemble your smashburgers.
